The Secrets of Temperature

Temperature is a measure of how hot or cold something is.The factors that affects temperature are altitude, latitude and distance from sea. The height measured from sea level is called altitude.latitude is a geographic coordinate that specifies the north–south position of a point on the Earth's surface. Today we are going to explore how altitude and latitude affect temperature on the earth.

Latitude

Tinker: Tinker with the range bar to drag the red star around the edge of the earth to change its latitude.Observe how the temperature varies with the latitude.

Equator

Earth is warmer near the equator because the equator receives energy from the sun at a more direct angle than the rest of the planet.

The North/South Pole

Both the Arctic (North Pole) and the Antarctic (South Pole) are 90 degrees latitude. They are very cold because they don't get any direct sunlight.
